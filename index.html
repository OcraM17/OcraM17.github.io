Hey there! I'm Marco, a 27-year-old Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Pavia, Italy. My thesis is about analyzing and developing deep and reinforcement learning algorithms for image enhancement. Pretty cool right? I'm into a bunch of different areas in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Image Processing, like Continual Learning, Neural Networks, Self-Supervised Learning, Image Translation and Generation. When I'm not busy nerding out about computer science, I love sports, especially soccer and basketball. I'm also a nature lover and traveller. I'm currently based in Pavia, Italy, a university city near Milan. In March 2022, I lived for 6 months in the amazing Barcelona, Spain, where I was a research intern at the Computer Vision Center. Before that, I spent 6 months in Ljubljana, Slovenia, a breathtaking city surrounded by nature. Here is where I worked on my Master's thesis and even though it was March 2020, I still managed to get it done while working half the time from home! So, that's a quick rundown of who I am and what I'm up to. I hope you find my journey and research as exciting as I do
